Deshangria's Grand Forge

Nobody is beating us when it comes to crafting armor, tech and magic. When it comes to our forge we're the best there is. our blacksmith creates weapons for kings, and champion class warriors alike. Not only them but also pieces for wizards and other beings. If there's anything you need this is the place to go. If you end up living here you'll get a nice discount too!

The Grand Forge opens from first bell until sundown, every day of the week.

What Our Forge Offers

🗡️

Weapons

Any weapon you can dream, we can make. We've made things from "The Fists of Thragg" which helped a legendary hero take down a dragon, to Ridlphine's Wings which was used to help the King of the kingdom of solitude escape a sinking ship. We got it all.

Here's some interesting blade making history if you're interested Wikipedia's Sword article.

🛡️

Armor

While we make great weapons, they're no use if you're stuck down before you can use them. Thank fully we make the best armor in the kingdom as well. Every generation of kings for The Kingdom of Solitude request us to make them their royale armor, and every year without fail they live long enough to be taken out by sickness rather than war.

🔧

Tools & Repairs

We don't just do big ticket items tho. We make your everyday day to day repair tools. So even if you are just trying to live a peaceful life and need some equipment we still got you.

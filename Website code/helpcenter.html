Deshangria Help Center

New to the city? Have a question about city services, hunting permits, the weekly schedule, or anything else? You've come to the right place. Our help center exists so no newcomer ever feels lost in Deshangria.

Browse the quick answers below, or fill out the contact form and a city representative will respond via message scroll within two business days.

FQA (Frequently Asked Questions)

🏠 Where do I register as a new resident?

New residents register at the City Gate office during the first three days of their arrival. Bring proof of your trade or skill and a letter of intent if you have one. Registration is free and grants you access to the meal hall, public events, and the library.

🐺 How do I get a hunting permit?

Hunting permits are issued at the City Guard post near the North Gate, you do have to pass a training first though. They are free for registered residents and valid for one season. Permits must be renewed each season. See the Hunting page for more details on what's allowed.

☀️ What are this week's rest days?

Rest days rotate by profession group and week. Check the bulletin board near the meal hall for the current week's schedule, or use the contact form below to ask a city representative directly.

🎭 How do I reserve theater seats?

Most showings are open-seating arrive early for popular performances. The Student Showcase and special events may require advance registration. Check the Theater page for current availability.

⚒️ Can I commission custom armor?

Yes! the Grand Forge accepts commission orders. Speak directly with Master Smith Gorrund during forge hours. Lead time is typically two to four weeks depending on complexity.

📜 Where can I learn more about city history?

The city library holds extensive archives, and this website's History page covers the major events. The Kingdom Archives are also available online for deeper research.

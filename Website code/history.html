The History of Deshangria

Deshangria is the first city in the Kingdom of Solitude to form, one of the original 7 civilizations which came into existance an eon ago. It has always been a pioneer of technological advancements, guided by the goddess Raliphina, who encourages evolution through science and the welcoming of all living beings. Now it stands as one of only 3 original civilizations still thriving, continuing to advance the world's technology through innovation, now and forever. Guess we really are too amazing since we're still here huh.

Some big innovations

Deshangria's scholars and craftspeople have created some of the best technologies ever:

Method of Foraging Silver

Deshangrian miners found a way to forge for silver, before silver was to tough to break with any current method. Thanks to the three amigos of Deshangria, it's now possible to collect silver.

Cannons

After the great siege one of the first new weapons created were cannons. This addition to the forces of Deshangria set their power miles above any other competing army.

Balloon Landers

A type of machine created during the great siege. It allows the user to fly high in the sky without the use of magic. This way magicless beings can soar high in the skies too

Ghost Flames

Although it's origins remain a mystery. A citizen from Deshangria created the ghost flame. This flame is said to produce warmth without causing harm to the touch. Though it's uses are many, the resources required to produce the encantation to create it out weigh the benefit. It's uses remain only for religious purposes.

The Great Siege

A picture of the moments before the battle

Not many armies have attempted to besiege Deshangria — the mountain terrain is treacherous enough to turn back most forces before they ever reach the walls. Those who do try always leave with a decisive defeat. But 327 years ago, one army came dangerously close to winning.

The Green Skins took a different approach. Rather than assaulting the walls directly, they razed the crop areas over several months, cutting off the food supply. They captured and killed every messenger sent to seek help. At this time, Deshangria had little formal army the mountains had always been defense enough.

With civilians dying of starvation and the Green Skins at the gates, a being named Stratos created balloon technology to build a ballooner and flew directly over the green skins to reach the next city for help. Allies arrived just in time for Deshangria to sally out it's forces and meet the green skins right outside the gates.

After that day, Deshangria vowed to never rely solely on terrain again. The King maintains a standing army trained in both traditional combat and advanced tactical strategy, a tradition upheld to this day.

King Brokunitus & How the Current Law Came to be

From the city's founding, Deshangria was ruled by the Trishola family, a monarchy that held power for generations. That is, until King Brokunitus Trishola.

Brokunitus was an ambitious and stubborn king who wished to redirect Deshangria's technological talent toward weapons and expansion, to conquer neighboring sister cities rather than cooperate with them. Most citizens were firmly opposed to becoming aggressors. The king, however, heard no answer except his own.

When his plot to start a war with a neighboring city was foiled by an unknown being, public outrage forced a change in the laws of governance. A new rule was established:

"After two generations, the citizens of Deshangria shall vote, either yes to hold an election for a new ruler, or no to wait another two generations. If An election is called it shall last one month and shall be open to all citizens to choose a new ruling family. Any family of Deshangria may enter, but they must have at least one current hier"

— The Brokunitus Accord, Year 891 of the Second Age

This system remains in place today, giving the citizens of Deshangria a voice that no other city in the Kingdom can claim.

Current Leadership

King Junaastio Liviticus

The current ruler of Deshangria, King Junaastio Liviticus, is widely regarded as one of the finest leaders the city has seen since its founding. With great ambition for technological progress and a deep respect for the citizens he serves, King Liviticus continues the tradition of pushing Deshangria forward, ensuring it remains not just the oldest city in the Kingdom, but the greatest.

Under his reign, the balloon lander program has been expanded, new aqueduct channels have been opened to the eastern farmlands, and the city's schools have received substantial investment — contributing to Deshangria's top ranking in education across the Kingdom.

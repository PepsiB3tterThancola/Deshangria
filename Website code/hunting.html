Field Notes

🐺 Mountain Wolf

The most common creature in the mountain passes, wolves travel in packs of four to twelve. A lone wolf is rarely a serious threat to an armed hunter, but packs should be approached with caution. Their pelts are warm and sell well at market.

Tip: Wolves are most active at dawn and dusk. A campfire will keep them at a distance overnight.

🦕 Mountain Dinosaur

A rare but formidable creature that inhabits the deep valleys east of the city. Hunting one is considered a rite of passage among Deshangrian warriors. Their hides are prized by the blacksmith for reinforcing armor plating.

Tip: Never approach from downwind. These creatures have an extraordinary sense of smell.

Learn more about these creatures here Wikipedia's animal article.

🦣 Wooly Mammoth

The woolly mammoths of the northern snowfields are massive but generally non-aggressive unless provoked or cornered. A successful mammoth hunt can feed a family for months and yields enough wool to outfit an entire household for winter.

Tip: A mammoth hunt requires patience. Set traps the night before and approach at midday when they are slowest.

Hunting Rules & Permits

What You Need

Before you hunt, you must have:

A valid hunting permit (contact the help center for more information)· A registered weapon (visit the Blacksmith for registration) · Declared your intended hunting zone to the City Guard. We just need to know for your safety and the safety of those around you.

What Is Protected

The mountain eagles and stone foxes are protected species and must not be hunted. Violations are taken seriously by the Crown. While there are other protected speices, you'll learn about in the training those two must NEVER be hunted as it is a crime punishable by death.

Hunting Parties for Hire

If you're new to Deshangria or the mountains, several experienced hunting parties accept new members looking for experience (they charge their own price). This is strongly recommended before attempting dinosaur or mammoth hunts alone.

Find a party via the Help Center.
